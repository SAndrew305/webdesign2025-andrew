Locally vs. in the cloud - Whats the difference Basically summed down to this: Cloud Pros - Fast - Scalable - Cost-effective if you don't have high-end hardware. - Access to new, pre-trained models - Ease of Use Cons - Can get expensive - Closed source and proprietary - Vendor Lock-In - Model Providers can use your chats to train their models - Requires Wi-Fi - Bad for the enviroment Local Pros - More control - Low costs - Excellent Privacy - Can be run offline - Less enviromental impact Cons - High costs upfront - Can be difficult to set up. - Limited Scalability - Can't access pre-trained models like (GPT-5)
